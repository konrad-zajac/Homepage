WCAG 2.0

The WCAG 2.0 is only a guideline, relying on it will not guarantee an accessible website. It does a great job of specifying what should be included, and is used in many machine auditing tools (like google lighthouse)but without the help of a real auditor it can make many "false positives", that is when an algorithm qualifies a requirement to be passed by the site but a real person reports it is not complete.
